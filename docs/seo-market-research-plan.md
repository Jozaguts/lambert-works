# LambertWorks SEO Market Research Plan

Date: 2026-05-09
Business: LambertWorks
Base location: 821 Cherry Ln, Plymouth Meeting, PA 19462
Primary conversion: estimate request by form or WhatsApp
Research method: `.codex/market-research` + `.codex/seo`

## Executive Summary

The local search surface around Plymouth Meeting is already competitive for broad handyman terms. Competitors are not only using homepage copy; they are creating service-location pages such as `Drywall Repair in Plymouth Meeting`, `Handyman Services in Plymouth Meeting, PA`, painting/carpentry pages, and basement-specific location pages.

Recommendation: LambertWorks should compete first with focused service + location content, not broad generic handyman copy. The first content push should target four practical topics: handyman repair lists, drywall repair, basement finishing/remodeling, and deck/patio/exterior repairs around Plymouth Meeting and nearby towns.

Positioning update: promote `honey-do list` service as the homeowner-friendly entry point for bundled small repairs. Use it as a practical phrase for repair lists, weekend projects, and household fixes without relying on outdated gender assumptions.

## Key Findings

### Fact: Competitors Use Service-Location Pages

Competitors are targeting specific pages around service + geography:

- `Drywall Repair in Plymouth Meeting, PA`
- `Handyman Services in Plymouth Meeting, PA`
- `Handyman in Plymouth Meeting, Pennsylvania`
- Basement waterproofing/foundation pages for Plymouth Meeting

This means LambertWorks should not rely only on a homepage section if the goal is organic visibility.

### Fact: Competitors Lead With Trust Signals

Competitor pages commonly lead with trust signals such as:

- years of experience
- Google reviews
- pricing examples
- free quote language
- licensed/insured or guarantee claims
- phone/WhatsApp CTAs

LambertWorks currently has stronger real project imagery than many generic pages, but the site needs more location and service-specific content depth over time.

### Fact: The Search Area Is Broader Than Plymouth Meeting

Competitors mention or target nearby areas including:

- Blue Bell
- Bridgeport
- Conshohocken
- West Conshohocken
- Lafayette Hill
- Whitemarsh Township
- Montgomery County

LambertWorks should use these naturally as service-area modifiers, especially in blog copy and future article pages.

### Inference: Drywall and Painting Are the Fastest Content Opportunities

Drywall repair appears repeatedly across competitor offers and connects naturally to painting, sanding, wall prep, caulking, trim, and small repairs. This is a strong early SEO angle because it has clear homeowner intent and can convert into estimate requests.

### Inference: Basement Content Should Avoid Foundation/Waterproofing Competition

Basement waterproofing and foundation repair competitors are more specialized. LambertWorks should position basement content around usable space, drywall, trim, paint, repair, storage, and finishing work, not structural or waterproofing claims.

## Implications

- Focus on service + location combinations.
- Use `honey-do list handyman` as a conversion-focused angle for bundled household repairs.
- Mention nearby service areas naturally: Plymouth Meeting, Whitemarsh Township, Lafayette Hill, Conshohocken, Blue Bell, Norristown, and Montgomery County.
- Tie each article to a real homeowner problem and a clear estimate CTA.
- Use real project photos from `src/assets/images` instead of stock-like blog imagery.
- Use the blog cards now, but create real article URLs next. Blog cards alone are not enough for long-term SEO.
- Add more trust proof over time: real reviews, project captions, service-area language, and before/after project details.

## Risks and Caveats

- Some competitor claims may change; this document is based on pages checked on 2026-05-09.
- Blog carousel cards are useful for relevance but weaker than crawlable article pages with titles, H1s, meta descriptions, and schema.
- Avoid keyword stuffing. Local modifiers should appear naturally in headings, excerpts, FAQs, and calls to action.
- Do not claim specialized services such as foundation repair, waterproofing, plumbing, or electrical unless LambertWorks truly offers them and can support them operationally.
- Ranking for broad `handyman Plymouth Meeting PA` may take longer because competitors already have dedicated location pages and review signals.

## Recommendation

Phase 1, already started:

- Replace generic blog topics with four local SEO cards.
- Use real project imagery.
- Point each card toward the contact/estimate action.
- Save this research as a repo document for future decisions.

Phase 2, recommended next:

- Add `LocalBusiness` schema on the homepage and `BlogPosting` schema on article pages.
